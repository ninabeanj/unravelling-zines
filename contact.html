Author Bio


Nina Jacobson is an artist and researcher currently based in Besançon, France. With a background in Fine Arts from Wits University and a focus on printmaking, Nina's work moves between traditional artistic practices and contemporary research in digital humanities. As part of a Master's in Rare Book and Digital Humanities at the Université de Bourgogne Franche-Comté, their research delves into the history and cultural significance of zines. Through a combination of hands-on artistic practice and academic inquiry, Nina seeks to illuminate the enduring relevance of zines. Their unique perspective as both an artist and scholar allows them to explore the intersection of creativity, history, and activism in meaningful ways.
